About Us

Who we are

We are a charming prenatal and postnatal wellness spa, conveniently located in Warwick, Rhode Island. We specialize in many areas pertaining to motherhood and beyond, from 3D/4D imaging to therapeutic massage.

Our intention is to provide you with a warm and inviting space along with nourishing educational and professional services as you journey through motherhood and beyond.

We are a group of healthcare professionals eager to serve you and your family. Your satisfaction is our number one priority. We are dedicated to capturing a moment that you can remember forever.

We offer numerous services to empower, educate, and pamper you along the way. As the physical and emotional needs of your pregnancy change, let Babies & Bellies be there for you! Enjoy your journey!

Our Staff

Devin Winkler

Hello! My name is Devin and I am the owner of Babies and Bellies 3D Ultrasound. I took over Babies and Bellies, originally founded by my Mom, when I had my first baby and realized I needed to make a career change that would allow me to be with him for all the important moments and milestones. For me, becoming a mother was a completely transformative process that left me in complete awe of mothers and women in general. I am now expecting my second baby boy and continue to be totally in tune with the needs and wants of expectant mothers.

I love to offer a service that allows mothers-to-be an experience that connects them to their baby before they are able to meet. For some, our services offer relief from worry. For others, it can be a celebration with friends and family. We hope you have a positive experience from start to finish when working with Babies and Bellies!

Deanna Parsons

Deanna is our lead ultrasound technician and has been with us since 2011. She first became interested in the ultrasound field while attending her own ultrasounds for her children and while pregnant with her 2nd child she decided to take the leap and follow her dreams. She graduated from a diagnostic medical ultrasound program in New York, NY in 2008.

Her first job after graduation was at a 3D ultrasound facility in NY where she developed her passion for obstetric ultrasound. She relocated to Rhode Island in 2010 and was determined to find a job doing what she loves most and fate brought her to Babies and Bellies!

Michelle Galipeau

Michelle attended CCRI in Lincoln, RI. She graduated in 1986 receiving an associate’s degree in Radiology. She was hired at a local community hospital as an x-ray technician working in diagnostic imaging. The ultrasound department was nearby and it wasn’t long before she sparked an interest in sonography.

She soon found herself back at school for ultrasound at the urging of a fellow colleague. Throughout her schooling, she remained employed by the same community hospital changing from a position in x-ray to a position in ultrasound in 1993.

She became registered by the ARDMS in abdominal and OBGYN ultrasound in 1994. She has always found scanning obstetrics one of the most rewarding areas to work in. It’s what originally sparked her interest in the ultrasound field. She started her position at Babies and Bellies in 2021 and is thrilled to be working with an exceptional team doing what she loves.

Kayla Wallace RDMS, RVT

Kayla Graduated from New England Institute of Technology in 2010, receiving an associates of science. She was hired at her externship site, a busy OBGYN office, where she worked as a medical assistant.

She enrolled in Rhode Island college a few years later initially to pursue a bachelors in nursing, but quickly realized her heart was in medical imaging. Working in the Obgyn office ignited her passion for ultrasound. She graduated from Rhode Island college and the Lifespan School of Medical Imaging in 2019.

She joined Babies and Bellies shortly after graduation in 2019 and loves being able to help families make great memories together.

Teagan Marble

Hi there! My name is Teagan and I work the front desk at Babies and Bellies. I’m currently in school to become a social worker! Some of my hobbies include crafting, reading, and painting. In fact, you’ll likely find me reading a book when you come in. I look forward to meeting you and your baby!

Emily Baker

Emily is an administrator at Babies & Bellies. She holds a Master’s degree in Social Work and worked for close to a decade in supporting families experiencing homelessness. Now, as mom of two young boys, Emily's main focus is being present with her own family. She still brings that caring and supportive energy into a new setting- at Babies & Bellies. She loves getting to know our families and creating a supportive environment for every stage of pregnancy.
