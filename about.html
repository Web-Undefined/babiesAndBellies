About Us

Who we are

We are a charming prenatal and postnatal wellness spa, conveniently located in Warwick, Rhode Island. We specialize in many areas pertaining to motherhood and beyond, from 3D/4D imaging to therapeutic massage.

Our intention is to provide you with a warm and inviting space along with nourishing educational and professional services as you journey through motherhood and beyond.

We are a group of healthcare professionals eager to serve you and your family. Your satisfaction is our number one priority. We are dedicated to capturing a moment that you can remember forever.

We offer numerous services to empower, educate, and pamper you along the way. As the physical and emotional needs of your pregnancy change, let Babies & Bellies be there for you! Enjoy your journey!

Our Staff

Lynn Butler

My name is Lynn. I’m the proud owner of Babies & Bellies 3D/4D Ultrasound Studio and Prenatal Wellness Spa. My vision was to always offer a family friendly environment with a caring and dedicated staff who are willing to go above and beyond to ensure your visit is comfortable and unforgettable. We take pride in all our new mommies-to-be, making sure they feel special and cared for during their visit.

At Babies & Bellies we truly value being able to bring you and your loved ones an experience that will last a lifetime. Seeing your new baby for the first time is such a magical experience. We are honored to have served thousands of families and are excited to share this Miracle of Life with you and your families.

We offer you the most advanced and up to date imaging system with our GE Voluson. All of our ARDMS or ARDMS eligible sonographers have been extensively trained in the art of 3D/4D imaging. At Babies & Bellies we are excited to bring you this new ultrasound technology and offer it to you as a wonderful bonding experience that the entire family will enjoy.

We are an established imaging facility that was originally founded in Providence, Rhode Island in 2010, but since moved to Warwick, Rhode Island July of 2018.

We offer a spacious, comfortable place for you to come and experience the miracle of life! We offer many entertainment 2D/ 3D and 4D ultrasound packages to fit every budget and we look forward to welcoming you!

Deanna Parsons

Deanna is our lead ultrasound technician and has been with us since 2011. She first became interested in the ultrasound field while attending her own ultrasounds for her children and while pregnant with her 2nd child she decided to take the leap and follow her dreams. She graduated from a diagnostic medical ultrasound program in New York, NY in 2008.

Her first job after graduation was at a 3D ultrasound facility in NY where she developed her passion for obstetric ultrasound. She relocated to Rhode Island in 2010 and was determined to find a job doing what she loves most and fate brought her to Babies and Bellies!

Michelle Galipeau

Michelle attended CCRI in Lincoln, RI. She graduated in 1986 receiving an associate’s degree in Radiology. She was hired at a local community hospital as an x-ray technician working in diagnostic imaging. The ultrasound department was nearby and it wasn’t long before she sparked an interest in sonography.

She soon found herself back at school for ultrasound at the urging of a fellow colleague. Throughout her schooling, she remained employed by the same community hospital changing from a position in x-ray to a position in ultrasound in 1993.

She became registered by the ARDMS in abdominal and OBGYN ultrasound in 1994. She has always found scanning obstetrics one of the most rewarding areas to work in. It’s what originally sparked her interest in the ultrasound field. She started her position at Babies and Bellies in 2021 and is thrilled to be working with an exceptional team doing what she loves.
